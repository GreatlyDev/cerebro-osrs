# Combat progression and boss readiness

Explanatory guidance for boss readiness, unlock burden, gear consistency, and repeatable combat routing.

Bossing advice should weigh unlock burden, route comfort, survivability, and repeatability rather than only raw combat stats. Barrows, Fight Caves, and similar activities feel much better once the account can repeat them cleanly. Slayer unlock advice should favor leverage per point spent, especially when it improves task value, convenience, or later profit.

Some combat routes also sit right on the line between PvM progression and money making. Activities like Demonic gorillas only feel truly good once the account can support their unlock burden and execution rhythm, so they should be compared against calmer repeatable routes instead of only by their best-case profit.
